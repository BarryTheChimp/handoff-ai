## Decisions

### DECISION-001: Project Name - Handoff AI

**Date:** December 2024  
**Status:** Accepted  
**Deciders:** Gary

**Context:**  
Needed a name that captures what the tool does - bridging the gap between product specs and developer work.

**Decision:**  
Named the project "Handoff AI" - emphasising the handoff moment between product and engineering.

**Rationale:**  
- "Handoff" is universally understood
- Describes the exact pain point
- Professional but not corporate
- Works well with Toucan Labs brand

**Consequences:**  
- Clear positioning
- Easy to explain to stakeholders
